A young woman, aged 24 ,from Liverpool UK started with music on the age of 16.
She got the inspiration for Folk-Rock music by her father, who was also a musician
and started making music round the sixties and seventies when Folk-Rock
was developed. Artists like The Byrds, Steeleye Span, Jimmy Hendricks, Pentangle
and Sandy Denny where inspirational for the British Folk-Rock what influenced
Goldie Snow to continue in this music style. Goldie worked with her father
and started composing her own songs,which led to 4 albums named:
Childhood memories, Lovely melody, Snow and Road trip.
She sings a lot about the past with warm feelings and what the future and her daily
life will bring. Her appearance makes you feel that she comes from the sixties
because her look and style are all based on the sixties, Goldie is a very warm
and a gentle person is eager to see what the future will bring for her and her fans!
We are so thrilled to announce her on our Crossed label night and can’t wait
to introduce her to all of you!

Say hello to The flecks, the two brothers from Texas (USA) that have been
inspired by folk rock ever since they were kids and now proudly present
their work to the world. The two brothers always loved the charm that folk rock brought and
they started a band together.
After many years of practice and passion for Folk Rock they
finally got the chance to show what they are worth to the world.
The flecks is known for their realistic view on the world that they put in every song
they play, their main focus is what happens in the daily live and how people deal with
these situations, but also they joy you have with friends and family when you are
spending the night at the bar and enjoy everything you have.
The flecks stand for friendship, loyalty, equality, curiosity, kindness and strength and these
are the values they live by every single day and want to share with the world.
We are proud to announce that these brothers will join us
on our Crossed record night and spread their joy with us,
so make sure you don’t miss out on these two!

Meet Indigo Blue; a new upcomming artist in the folk rock scene.
With her 23 years on this earth she has managed to introduce
allready 4 albums! Indigo Blue is a young artist from Nashville,
Tennesee (U.S.A.) who started playing music in a folk band with
friends at the early age of 8. In this band she was the vocalist but
when they grew older she wanted to change things up an make a
switch to folk rock, the others weren’t in so she went solo.
Indigo is know for her fresh take on folk rock music.
She likes to use the traditional ellements but with a modern twist.
She feels like it’s definitly very important to treasure the classic basis
of a genre and respect your roots nor or less stay true to your own
style and take on things by not being affraid of adding new ellements
without breaking it. This is a very complex task that could easily break
the folk rock vibe, that’s why her succes in doing this so gently makes
her the singer to watch out for these comming years! We are so glad
to announce that also Indigo Blue will be attending our
Crossed Records night, make shure u don’t miss her!!
