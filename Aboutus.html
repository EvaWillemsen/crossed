We are a record label situated in Antwerp,Belgium.
We cover the music style Folk-Rock and work
internationally together with artists from the UK
and the US, but also with artists worldwide who play Folk-rock.
Our name is based on the music style Folk-rock with the meaning that the music is a mix from
folk and rock and is influenced by different instruments.
Our logo is visualised as strings of a guitar because thats one of the most commom instruments that’s in use,
therefore we are called “Crossed records”.
